ABOUT ME


Welcome to my personal blog. My given name is Yunzhe WANG. You may also address me by my Cantonese-inspired phonetic name, Wenzy WONG. In this blog, you'll find my random thoughts on scientific research, my tiny academic contributions, some notes I would like to share, and poems I wrote just for fun (since 2025). Below is a brief summary of my educational background and academic journey over the past few years.

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Bioinformatics at College of Informatics, Huazhong Agricultural University, where I systematically learned this field and developed both data-driven and question-driven thinking. Beyond traditional undergraduate studies, to compensate for the lack of exposure to medical research at an agricultural university, I also participated in a visiting research programme, Biological and Biomedical Science Programme, at School of Life Sciences, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. The group I led there won the second price in the ending presentation of this programme.

I am currently a third-year student in the combined Master's and Ph.D. programme at Zhang Lab, School of Basic Medical Sciences, Fudan University. During my Ph.D. programme, I have continued to enhance bioinformatics skills, aiming to understand more of their effective and reasonable applications in cancer-related studies. My current research focuses on utilizing multi-omics data to explore unique microbial communities associated with human gastrointestinal tract cancers. I am also deeply interested in interdisciplinary fields, such as the relationship between neuroactive substances and cancer, the impacts of metabolites from various sources on epigenetic reprogramming, etc.
